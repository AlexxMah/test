Kenny doesn’t like to name-drop but since he’s writing this in the third person he feels a little more comfortable spewing out a laundry list of athletes, celebrities and icons he’s had the chance to work with:

Steph Curry, Jayson Tatum, Simone Biles, Baker Mayfield, Anna Kendrick, The Geico Caveman, Alice Cooper, Carli Lloyd, Anthony Davis, Sean Astin (Goonies never say die!), Odell Beckham Jr, The Orange M&M, Joel Embiid, Rob Gronkowski (who hasn’t?), Charles Barkley, Bill Russell, The Geico Gecko, Derek Jeter, Lonzo Ball, Jake from State Farm, Saquon Barkley, Derrik Rose, Josh Allen, Drew Brees, 3 different Masters champions, Russell Westbrook, Peter “Don’t call me Shirley” Graves, Kyrie Irvin, Eli Manning, Draymond Green, Justin Herbert, Julie and Zac Ertz, Klay Thompson, George Kittle, Kerri Walsh Jennings, Manny Pacquiao, Michelle Williams (Destiny’s Child), Nick Lachey (98 Degrees), Damian Lillard, Darth Vader, Tony Romo, Karl-Anthony Towns, The Florida Gator, Carmelo Anthony, Pat Mahomes, Stephen A. Smith, Scott Van Pelt (Go Terps!), Admiral Akbar (yes that Admiral Akbar), Kawhi Leonard, Tim Duncan, De’Aaron Fox, Devin Booker, Joakim Noah, Jimmy Kimmel, Dennis Haysbert, Paulie Walnuts, Jon Cena and Clive Owen.

Kenny is also way too humble to mention that he’s won Cannes Lions, D&AD and One Show awards as both a writer and director.
